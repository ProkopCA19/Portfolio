Some tips for success...





  1. Know when not to engage in a fight! Dont pick fights when you dont have to
  2. Landing in a spot across the map gives you more time to loot without having to fight off other players
  3. Stay out of the storm & never stop moving.Jumping while running makes it harder for snipers to get you.
  4. Dont go immediately go for your opponents loot- scope out your surroundings first
  5. Wood is good to use in high pressure situations, if you have more time you should build your fort out of metal.
